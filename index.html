What is BIS certification, and why is it important for my business?

BIS certification, provided by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), is a quality certification mark that signifies that a product meets the standards set by the BIS. For businesses, obtaining BIS certification is crucial as it ensures that their products adhere to stringent quality and safety standards, enhancing product reliability and consumer trust.
